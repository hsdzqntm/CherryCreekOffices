Property Deep Dives
1 240 St Paul St — Equinox Building (Gart Properties)

Building: Cherry Creek's newest office building, completed 2021 by BMC Investments. 6-story building with two underground parking levels. Now owned by Gart Properties, who occupy corporate headquarters on 2nd floor. Designed by Open Studio Architecture with distinctive panelized facade.

The Space: 5th-floor sublease at 4,756 RSF. 14 private offices (12 standard + 2 executive), one conference room, kitchenette, reception, elevator identity. Fully furnished plug-and-play condition. Available immediately through October 31, 2031.

Parking: Valet parking with underground automatic car stackers. Secured in-building.

Ground Floor: Polestar electric vehicle showroom. SoulCycle (Equinox-owned brand) operates directly across the street.

Pricing: Stream Realty lists at "upon request." Expect upper end of Cherry Creek sublease rates given 2021 vintage and AA finishes. Space has been on market since April 2024 (~2 years) which creates significant negotiating leverage.

Critical: There is NO Equinox gym. The building was named for an Equinox fitness club that was supposed to anchor floors 1-3 with 33,000 SF of gym space (Equinox's first Mountain West location). COVID intervened; lease terminated July 2022. No showers, lockers, or fitness facilities for tenants.
2 100 Fillmore St — Fillmore Place (SE Fillmore Place LLC)

Building: 6-story building, built 1982, renovated 2014. Distinctive sawtooth design creates numerous corner offices with private balconies — an extraordinarily rare amenity in Cherry Creek. Floor-to-ceiling windows on every level.

Available Suites (per brochure): Suite 225 (2nd fl, 6,172 SF), Suite 400 (4th fl, 3,428 SF), Suite 410 (4th fl, 1,903 SF), Suite 450 (4th fl, 4,539 SF), Suite 455 (4th fl, 1,017 SF), Suite 500 (5th fl, 15,030 SF), Suite 600 (6th fl, 13,973 SF). Best fit: Suite 450 at 4,539 SF.

Amenities: Fitness center (confirmed), bike storage, 24-hour security with manned reception. Directly adjacent to Cherry Creek Mall (<1 min walk). Walk Score 92, Bike Score 92.

Pricing: $73/SF full-service (highest in set). Reserved parking $200/mo, unreserved $135/mo.

Co-Tenants: Regus (5th floor coworking), Arrowpoint Asset Management, Bank of America.

Availability Discrepancy: Research report found NAI Shames Makovsky's current listing shows 2nd-floor suites available January 2027, but brochure shows 4th-6th floor suites. Must confirm whether upper-floor balcony suites are genuinely available and timeline.
3 44 Cook St, Suite 650 — First Avenue Plaza (Granite Properties)

Building: 10-story Class A tower in the two-building First Avenue Plaza complex (264,274 SF total). Owned by Granite Properties in 50/50 JV with Long Wharf Capital, acquired 2022 for $105 million. Significant renovation 2017-2019: new exterior, updated marble/granite lobby, new elevators, purpose-built tenant amenity center.

The Space: Suite 650 on 6th floor, 3,824 RSF. Sublease from Overland Oil and Gas. Likely move-in ready with existing professional office buildout.

Amenities: Boardroom, training room, collaboration areas, free coffee service, men's/women's locker rooms with showers, dedicated bike storage, outdoor plaza with fireplace between the two buildings.

Co-Tenants: BlackRock Cachematrix (8th floor), Vinson & Elkins (major law firm), FMI Capital Advisors.

Views: 6th-floor position offers decent western mountain views, though not penthouse-level panoramas.

Verify: Sublease pricing not publicly listed. Confirm remaining term on Overland Oil & Gas master lease, furniture inclusion, and move-in readiness.
4 55 Madison St, Suite 550 — First Avenue Plaza (Granite Properties)

Building: Companion 8-story building to 44 Cook in the same First Avenue Plaza complex (137,046 SF). Classified as Class B on LoopNet (brick-and-glass exterior vs. Cook's aluminum curtain wall).

The Space: Suite 550 on 5th floor, 3,406 RSF. New spec-suite buildout: 5 offices, 1 conference room, 4 workstations. NOT ready until approximately October 2026. Expandable to 4,864 SF by combining with Suite 520.

Pricing: $41/SF NNN. Shares the same First Avenue Plaza amenity package as 44 Cook.

Views: 5th floor of 8 stories provides limited mountain views compared to higher positions in the taller sister building.

Verify: Confirm whether this is a sublease or direct spec-suite lease (LoopNet suggests the latter). Nail down completion date — "approximately 10/1/2026" is vague.
5 50 S Steele St — Steele Park (The Broe Group)

Building: 10-story building with penthouse. Built 1974, renovated 2012/2013 and 2021. Owned by The Broe Group (private, multi-billion-dollar Denver investment firm) since 1988. Sits directly across from Cherry Creek Mall.

Available Space (per brochure): Suite 200 at 6,575 SF on 2nd floor. Brochure also mentions "narrow bay depths to accommodate smaller users" and "multiple move-in ready options" but only Suite 200 is detailed in floor plans.

Class: Disputed — Broe markets as Class A, but LoopNet and CBRE list as Class B. Given 1974 vintage and amenity set, B/B+ is more accurate.

Amenities: Showers, cafe/restaurant, conference facility, controlled access. No gym, no outdoor terrace, no tenant lounge.

Views: Penthouse offers "stunning mountain views." Upper floors command strong Front Range sightlines from ~100+ feet.

Parking: Cheapest in the set at $65-100/month with 2.80 ratio.

Verify: Building undergoing re-measurement — final RSF approximate. Confirm whether smaller suites within top floors available independently. Ask about Broe Group's temporary tenancy timeline.
6 205 Detroit St, Fl 7-8 — Financial House

Building: Arguably the most impressive building in this set. Striking all-glass, 8-story tower completed 2019, designed by 4240 Architects. Walk Score 94 (highest). Corner of 2nd Avenue and Detroit Street.

The Space: Penthouse floors 7 (5,939 SF) and 8 (9,869 SF), totaling 15,808 RSF. NOT divisible. Hardwood-floored, modern office space with interconnected stairwell. Panoramic views through floor-to-ceiling glass: unobstructed Front Range including Mount Evans, Longs Peak, and the Continental Divide.

Fatal Flaw: At 15,808 RSF, this is 3-4× what a 5-15 person team needs. Annual rent estimated at $630,000-$870,000.

Co-Tenants: UBS Financial Services (entire 5th floor), BMC Investments (developer). Ground-floor Amazon Books.

Amenities: Outdoor terrace with fire pit, bike storage, EV charging, on-site security.

Verify: Listing broker appears to be Roche & Company, not JLL. Confirm whether partial-floor arrangements possible. Verify if space comes furnished.
7 3300 E 1st Ave — Key Bank Plaza (GEM Realty Capital)

Building: 6-story Class B building, built 1978, renovated 2016. 97,770 RSF. Only property with Energy Star certification.

Best-Fit Suite: Suite 370 at 4,349 SF — spec-ready with 8 offices, 2 conference rooms, huddle room, reception, kitchen, open area. Also: Suite 230 (4,738 SF, whiteboxed), Suite 560 (1,400 SF, plug-n-play furnished).

OODA Life: Basement-level premium wellness club with cold plunges, red light therapy, AI-powered fitness. However: $750/month membership, separate tenant (not included).

Tenant Mix Concern: Heavily medical/dental (Aspen Dental, DeWitt Dental, Aesthetic Surgery & Dermatology) — may not project executive image.

Views: Limited from 6-story building surrounded by Cherry Creek development.

Verify: Request rent from CBRE (not disclosed). Clarify which suites spec-ready vs whitebox. Confirm parking costs. Ask about medical-tenant concentration and repositioning plans.

Notes & Definitions

  • RSF = Rentable Square Feet (includes pro-rata common areas). NNN = Triple Net (tenant pays operating expenses on top of base rent).
  • Full-service = rent includes operating expenses. Add $15-25/SF to NNN rents for apples-to-apples comparison.
  • Class AA/A/B: AA = premium finishes beyond Class A standard. A = high-quality professional. B = functional but dated or less prestigious.
  • Walk Score: 90+ = "Walker's Paradise." 70-89 = "Very Walkable." Source: WalkScore.com.
  • Scoring: Weighted average of 7 categories. Prestige, views, fitness, and move-in readiness weighted more heavily. Size Fit adjusted with actual brochure data.
  • Data sourced from listing broker marketing materials and independent research as of March 2026.
